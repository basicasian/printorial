> Chapter 1: 3D Printing in Vienna - Where to go
  • techLAB at Technisches Museum: Museum‑run makerspace with open‑access 3D printers (free with museum ticket/annual pass)
  • Happylab Wien: Large makerspace with 24/7 access and multiple 3D printers; membership required but free intro tours/workshops.
  • Maker Austria: Large, non-profit makerspace in Vienna with public access, offering FDM and SLA 3D printers, 24/7 member access, and a strong community for collaborative projects
  • MetaLab: Vienna’s renowned hackerspace with 3D printers, CNC, electronics, etc.
> Chapter 2: Reycling your Filament left-overs

If you have leftover filament, you can collect them and reycle them at Precious Plastic Vienna. You can drop off small quantities (up to 10 kg) at the WUK info desk (Währinger Straße 59, 1080 Vienna) during opening hours (Mon–Fri 9:00–20:00, Sat/Sun 15:00–20:00), packed in a bag or box. Please email info@preciousplastic.wien to let them know. For larger amounts, contact them to arrange a handover.
