About Printorial

Printorial is a beginner-friendly tutorial for setting up a 3D printer, starting from the basics of 3D printing to a step-by-step guide on how to print.
This project was done in the scope of the course Free and Open Technologies 2025SS of the Technical University of Vienna, overseen by Evropi Stefanidi.
